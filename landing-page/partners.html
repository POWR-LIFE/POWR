How It Works

Simple for you.
Powerful for your members.

We add your venue to the app with your logo and partner profile. Approved and live within 24 hours.

02

POWR detects when members arrive via GPS. Seamless and passive. Nothing changes on your end.

03

Every visit builds their points balance. Streaks multiply rewards. Consistency becomes something worth protecting.

Retention, by design

Reward loops create habit. Members who earn on every visit have a concrete reason to keep coming back, turning irregular gym-goers into regulars.

Streaks drive attendance

Loss aversion is one of the strongest behavioural forces there is. Members don't want to break their streak. That pull increases visit frequency, not just intention.

The network effect

As POWR grows, so does your reach. Every new user is a potential member who already knows your name before they walk through the door.
